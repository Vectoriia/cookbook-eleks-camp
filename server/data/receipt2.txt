Shred the chicken, return it to the soup, and add cream:*While the apples and rice are cooking in the soup, shred the chicken, discarding any tough bits. Once the rice and apples in the soup are cooked, add the chicken back to the pot. Heat for 5 minutes more. Then stir in the cream.*6. Serve:*Serve with yogurt and chives.
