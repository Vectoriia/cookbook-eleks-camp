Hungarian Mushroom Soup|0|Hungarian|Soups|6|201|230|4 - tablespoon butter*3 - onion*1 - pound mushrooms*2 - teaspoon dill weed*1 - tablespoon sweet paprika*2 - teaspoon soy sauce*2 - cup chicken stock*1 - cup milk*3 - tablespoon flour*1 - teaspoon salt*0.5 - teaspoon black pepper*2 - teaspoon lemon juice*1 - tablespoon parsley*0.5 - cup sour cream|1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Saute the onions in the butter for 5 minutes. Add the mushrooms and saute for 5 more minutes. Stir in the dill, paprika, soy sauce and broth. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.*2. In a separate small bowl, whisk the milk and flour together. Pour this into the soup and stir well to blend. Cover and simmer for 15 more minutes, stirring occasionally.*3. Finally, stir in the salt, ground black pepper, lemon juice, parsley and sour cream. Mix together and allow to heat through over low heat, about 3 to 5 minutes. Do not boil. Serve immediately.
