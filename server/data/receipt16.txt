Ukrainian Dumplings aka Galushki|0|Ukrainian|Main dishes|6|371|250|5 - cup flour*4 - egg*1 - cup water*9 - tablespoon butter*1 - pound mushrooms*1 - onion*0.5 - pound bacon*1 - tablespoon olive oil*1.5 - teaspoon salt*0.5 - teaspoon black pepper|1. Place 5 cups of flour in a large mixing bowl. Create a well in the center of the flour. Whisk 4 eggs with 1 cup of lukewarm water and 150 grams of melted butter. Bring the dough together with a fork and finish mixing by hand.*2. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a log shape. Chop each log into smaller dumplings.3. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil. Add the dumplings to the saucepan and cook them until they float to the top of the water.*4. Place 1 pound of diced mushrooms in an oiled skillet and sauté them for about 5 minutes.*5. Add a diced onion and continue cooking until soft. Season to taste with salt and pepper.*6. Stir 1 cup of bacon pieces and 3 tablespoons of butter to the mushrooms in the skillet. Continue to cook for 5 more minutes.*7. Place the dumplings in a separate skillet and add the sautéed mixture. Cook until golden brown.*8. Serve the galushki immediately!
